AriaApollo

Aria Apollo Hospital was opened in 1386 with the approval of the Ministry of Public Health, and after five years of specialized treatment of children and internal diseases with Indian and Afghan doctors, and many successful efforts to expand the hospital into a wider center and create newer departments with advanced facilities in specialized medical services of urology, traumatology, pediatrics, general surgery, orthopedics, physiotherapy, EMG, dermatology and beauty, ear and throat. nose, eyes and teeth. Even now, our dear fellow citizens benefit from round-the-clock services in 30 beds in public and private rooms with an ambulance.
